调试九法：软硬件错误的排查之道

Oct 16, 2016 | code

这本书可算是一本写作方面的营销法则应用的典范。作者先作好铺垫—说明调试的重要性和他的九个法则的优点，然后再进行内容写作。

作者的九个调试规则如下：

理解系统：Understand the system – Read the manual, you can’t fix something unless you know how it is supposed to work.

对系统的掌握决定你对错误的了解。

制造失败：Make it fail – I have always used this one, but I called it, “If I can make it worse, I can make it better.

也就是重现（replay）错误。

不要想，而要看：Quit Thinking and Look – Don’t just think about the problem, add debugging, hook up a scope, get your hands dirty.

先观察，再思考。注意海森堡效应—观察可能会影响被观察的对象工作。

分而治之：Divide and Conquer – Keep dividing the search area in half, make it hard for the bug to hide.

缩小错误范围。

一次只改一个地方：Change One Thing at a Time – Don’t try to find bugs with a shotgun, use a rifle instead.

一个bug可由多个因素引起，一次调试多个bug不好确定原因。

保持审计跟踪：Keep an Audit Trail – Keep detailed notes on how you reproduced a bug, it makes it easier on everyone to find it and fix it.

记录调试过程非常重要。

检查插头：Check the Plug – Start at the beginning, don’t just start debugging where you think the bug might be.

检测最初的假设，它们可能是错的。

获得全新观点：Get a Fresh View – Run a difficult problem passed a colleague or even your mother. Talking fixes as many problems as the debugger does.

不要让思维固化，交流。

如果你不修复bug，它将依然存在：If You Didn’t Fix It, It Ain’t Fixed – You have to understand why your fix resolved the problem. If not, keep looking. David has a website, it hasn’t been updated in a while but it does have a nice PDF of the 9 Debugging Rules to hang on your wall.

不要无视bug，她不会自己消失。
